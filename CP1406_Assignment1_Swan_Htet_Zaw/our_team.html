Meet our team

At 100SMILES Dental Care, our dentists, hygienist and reception staff share a commitment to friendly, reliable and compassionate service. We look after patients of all ages and aim to make every visit as comfortable as possible.

Dr Johanna Grey

DDS, Orthodontics

Dr Johanna Grey focuses on orthodontic care for teenagers and adults. After graduating from a distinguished dental program, she has continued to refine her skills in aligning teeth and improving bites. Johanna enjoys helping patients feel more confident about their smiles.

Dr Benedict Sloan

DMD – General Dentist

Dr Benedict (Ben) Sloan is an experienced general dentist whose career has been devoted to overall dental health and wellbeing. He provides check-ups, restorative treatments and patient education, and is known for his calm, reassuring manner.

Richard Attam

BDS – Dental Hygienist

Richard Attam is our dedicated dental hygienist. With a strong focus on prevention, he carries out cleans, provides practical flossing and brushing tips, and supports the dentists in maintaining your long-term oral health.

April Kepner

Receptionist

April is the friendly voice you hear when you call the clinic. She looks after appointments, enquiries and reminders, and works hard to ensure that every visit runs smoothly from the moment you arrive.

Our approach to care

We believe that good dental care combines technical skill with kindness. Our team takes time to explain your treatment options and answer questions so you can make informed decisions.

Whether you are bringing a child for their first visit or booking a regular check-up for yourself, we want you to feel listened to, respected and supported.
