Our Dental Services

We provide a full spectrum of preventive and restorative care to keep your smile healthy and confident. From routine wellness visits to complex treatments, our experienced team will recommend options that fit your needs and budget.

Below is a summary of the main services available at 100SMILES Dental Care. If you have questions about a specific treatment, please contact us — we are happy to talk through your options.

Preventive care & check-ups

Regular check-ups are the foundation of good dental health. During these visits we:

  • Examine your teeth, gums and jaw for signs of disease or abnormalities.
  • Perform professional cleans to remove plaque and tartar.
  • Take X-rays when necessary to detect hidden problems early.
  • Provide personalised oral hygiene and nutritional guidance.

Annual Dental Check-Up package

Our Annual Dental Check-Up package combines several preventive services in one convenient visit:

  • Physical examination of your teeth, gums and mouth.
  • Professional cleaning to remove plaque and tartar.
  • X-rays where required to check for common dental issues.
  • Dental health review and treatment recommendations.
  • Nutritional guidance tailored to your needs.
  • Additional tests if recommended based on your history.

Book before 31 March to receive a 10% discount on the Annual Dental Check-Up package.

Restorative, implant & cosmetic dentistry

Restorative & implant dentistry

When teeth are damaged or missing, we offer restorative treatments such as fillings, crowns, bridges and dental implants. Our aim is to restore function while maintaining a natural appearance.

Cosmetic dentistry

Cosmetic options such as teeth whitening and aesthetic restorations can brighten and refine your smile. We focus on realistic improvements that suit your face and lifestyle.

Orthodontics

Our orthodontic services help straighten teeth and improve bite alignment. After an assessment, we can discuss braces or other options appropriate for teenagers and adults.

Emergency & acute dental care

Toothaches, broken teeth and accidents can happen at any time. We reserve time in our schedule for urgent cases, so please call us as soon as possible if you are in pain or have suffered a dental injury.

We will provide advice over the phone and arrange an emergency appointment where appropriate.
