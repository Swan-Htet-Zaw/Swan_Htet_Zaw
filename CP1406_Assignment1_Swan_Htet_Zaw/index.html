Why choose 100SMILES Dental Care?

Located at 33 Fantail Street in Razole, 100SMILES Dental Care has cared for the local community for more than three decades. With over 50 years of combined experience, our team focuses on preventive care, long-term relationships and clear communication so you always know what is happening with your dental health.

Care for every stage of life

We welcome children, busy adults and retirees. Whether you need a routine check-up, help with a dental emergency or advice about implants or braces, our dentists will tailor a plan that suits your needs.

Modern facility, gentle approach

Our clinic is equipped for diagnostics, treatments and surgery when needed. We emphasise gentle treatment, clear explanations and practical oral hygiene tips you can use at home.

Prevention first, for a healthy smile

At 100SMILES Dental Care we believe prevention is always better than cure. Regular check-ups, professional cleans and X-rays help us detect issues early, before they become painful or expensive problems.

During your visit we also discuss nutrition, daily brushing and flossing, and any changes you have noticed in your mouth or gums. Our goal is to help you feel confident about your smile and your long-term oral health.
